User Verification

Mobile Number Verification: Every user's contact number is verified by sending an OTP to the mobile number mentioned, to confirm the ownership of the number. And now that every mobile number is linked to Aadhaar, it adds another layer of security. Company Verification: We verify each user's profile with a verification link or an OTP sent to your organization email id. Also the company name and domain name of the email id should be same in order for the account to be verified. This ensures that they work for a corporate entity.

Route Deviation Alert

When the rider deviates from the pre planned route, the system sends an automatic emergency alert to your emergency contact as well as to the company.If the Emergency button is pressed during route deviation alert the details of the driver as well as the location of the car will be sent to the police

Emergency Button

You can tap the panic button to initiate an emergency alert that'll be sent to your emergency contact during the ride. The system will send a SMS with the location details to your emergency contact as well as the Police. The location will be sent periodically every 5 mins.

Track

The car can we Tracked while on-service or while using the carpool app.The car can also be tracked if a carpoolapp of rider is on-service with the a asoociated car.

Reject Offers

You can reject offer as well as reject the rides if you find the car in bad condition or you find the driver unsafe.The carpool Ride givers can also reject offers if the find the rider to be unsafe or rude acording to the opinion.

Overspeed Alert

When the driver is overspeeding on the route, the system sends an automatic overspeeding alert to the rider as wellas the driver .If the overspeeding alert does'nt go away in 2 mins then the driver gets fined for his next ride.
